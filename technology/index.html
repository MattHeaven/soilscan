Our Technology

SoilScan's technology represents the intersection of advanced AI, digital infrastructure, and modern agronomy. Our platform is designed to address the complexities of soil degradation through a seamless integration of soil health assessment, AI-driven insights, and customized fertilizer production, all supported by a global Soil Health Index Registry.

Soil Health Index Registry

This registry is a sophisticated digital platform that systematically catalogs comprehensive soil health data using a farm index for farms worldwide. It enables the precise tracking of soil health for each specific farm and ensures that each farm receives an independent NPK fertilizer formulation. The registry also enhances traceability throughout the supply chain, significantly reducing the risk of counterfeit fertilizers.

AI-Powered NPK Formulation

Our AI algorithms analyze the detailed soil health data captured in the registry, processing variables such as nutrient levels, pH, organic matter, and climatic conditions. These insights allow us to generate precise NPK fertilizer formulations that cater to the specific nutrient requirements of each farm, optimizing both soil health and crop productivity.

Climate-Responsive Crop Insurance

Leveraging the indexed soil and climate data, we provide farmers with access to tailored crop insurance products that mitigate the financial risks associated with climate variability. This insurance model is designed to offer reliable protection, ensuring that farmers can maintain their livelihoods even in adverse conditions.

Customized Fertilizer Production and Distribution

The AI-generated formulations are sent directly to our network of local manufacturers. These manufacturers produce fertilizers according to the specific needs identified by our algorithms, ensuring that each batch is perfectly matched to the soil health data of the farms it is intended for. The fertilizers are then distributed based on geographic proximity, and farmers use their unique index numbers to retrieve the products specifically designed for their soil. The traceability ensured by the index helps combat the proliferation of counterfeit fertilizers, safeguarding the integrity of the agricultural inputs.

How It Works

Our technology is designed to be user-friendly and accessible to farmers of all sizes. The process is simple and intuitive, allowing farmers to seamlessly integrate our platform into their existing operations.

Farm Indexing

Each farm is assigned a unique digital identity within the Soil Health Index Registry, making it easier to track soil health data and ensure the production of precise NPK formulations.

Soil Sampling and Digitization

Biannual soil testing is conducted, and the data is digitized and added to the farm's profile in the registry.

AI Analysis

AI algorithms analyze the soil data to generate precise NPK formulations tailored to each farm’s unique needs.

Transmission to Manufacturers

The AI-generated formulations are sent to manufacturers for production according to exact specifications, ensuring traceability and reducing the risk of counterfeit fertilizers.

Manufacturing

Fertilizers are produced based on the AI-generated formulations, ensuring they meet the specific needs of each farm and maintaining the integrity of the supply chain.

Distribution

Fertilizers are distributed to farmers, who use their unique index number to retrieve the products tailored to their soil, with the added assurance of product authenticity.

Application and Monitoring

Farmers apply the fertilizers, and ongoing monitoring ensures that future formulations are adjusted as needed, with continuous traceability throughout the process.
